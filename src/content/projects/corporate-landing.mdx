## Context

A landing page for an Italian company, conceived and built entirely by me — from initial design to production deployment. The company name and domain are withheld until public release.

The goal is a fast, accessible, and conversion-focused single-page site that clearly communicates the company's value proposition and drives the target action.

## Stack

Built with Angular and TypeScript, styled with Tailwind CSS. The stack was chosen for its structure and scalability: even a landing page benefits from a disciplined component model when content evolves frequently.

Early development. Project is currently in progress.
